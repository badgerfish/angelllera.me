How I work

At the same time I consider myself an aggressive generalist, meaning I can speak about any given subject in detail for about 10 minutes, and before you think that that doesn't seem like very much, try giving your opinion on contrastive focus reduplication without googling what it is. Sure I know programming and a couple of languages, but I also apply my knowledge of design, philosophy, ethics, logic, and even gaming to solve problems, which makes me slightly more interesting than that person sitting next to you, unless they funnier than me, which is unlikely. When I encounter a problem my first move is to try and understand what caused it, most of the time standard fixes aren't enough, whenever possible I like to fix and prevent problems, bugs, errors, and other obstacles; but more importantly I have a desite to understand why the happened in the first place. Sometimes I don't get along with other people, this is because other people sometimes make me anxious, specially in large groups. I get overwhelmed easily, specially if given a task with exceptionally loose tolerances, or lacking specific goals and requirements.
